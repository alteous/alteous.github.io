Music

I play the guitar. Nowadays I enjoy playing blues, particularly Stevie Ray Vaughan. I'd like to learn violin one day and play old time string band tunes.

I'm currently perfecting Cliffs of Dover by Eric Johnson on the electric guitar and playing AC/DC songs in the meantime. Dave Cartwright is my teacher.

Some of my favourite tunes to play are Scuttle Buttin' and Rude Mood by Stevie Ray Vaughan and Summer Song by Joe Satriani.

A few years ago I was more into fingerstyle guitar. I learned to play Rhett Butler's awesome arrangement of Cliffs of Dover and Larry Pattis' beautiful arrangement of Waltzing Matilda.
