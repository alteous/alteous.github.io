Music

I play the guitar. Nowadays I enjoy playing blues, particularly Stevie Ray Vaughan. I'd like to learn violin one day and play old time string band tunes.

I'm currently working on playing Scuttle Buttin' on the electric guitar. A few years ago I was more into fingerstyle guitar. I learned to play Rhett Butler's awesome arrangement of Cliffs of Dover and Larry Pattis' beautiful arrangement of Waltzing Matilda.
