Recommendations

  • Use duckduckgo.com to browse the web in peace.

  • Use redshift to allow your eyes adjust to using the computer during the evening.
  • Use the Tango colour palette (try (load-theme 'tango-dark) in emacs).
  • Remove blinking cursors to save your sanity.
  • Enable sticky keys for comfort and to avoid 'emacs pinky'.
  • If, like me, you have long dry hair: read my hair care guide.
